## ðŸŽ¨ Text Overlay Design

**Font:**
- Clean, modern sans-serif (e.g., Inter, Montserrat, or SF Pro)
- Bold weight for emphasis

**Colors:**
- White text with subtle shadow (for readability over cosmic theme)
- Or: Purple/blue gradient text matching app theme
- Background: Semi-transparent black box behind text (optional)

**Size:**
- Large enough to read on mobile (test at 480p)
- 72-96pt font size minimum

**Position:**
- Top third or bottom third of screen (avoid center where action happens)
- Consistent position throughout video

**Animation:**
- Fade in (0.3s)
- Stay on screen (as per timestamps above)
- Fade out (0.3s)
- OR slide in from bottom, slide out to top
